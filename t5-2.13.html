LilyGO TTGO T5 Epaper 2.13 inch ESP32 Board

In the box:
  • 1x LilyGO TTGO T5 board
  • 2x 13 pin male headers
  • 1x 2 pin 1.25mm JST connector with wires (for connecting to a 3.7v rechargeable lithium battery
Features:
  • Use Arduino IDE or Platformio with ESP32 libraries to write sketches to run on the T5
  • Great for IoT projects that utilise WiFi, Bluetooth and the board's range of digital and analogue I/O.
  • Very low power consumption in sleep mode, and the epaper display won't use any power while it isn't being updated. Can charge a lithium ion battery from its USB micro-B port.
  • Built-in Micro SD card slot.

FT232RL USB to serial UART interface

In the box:
  • 1x FT232RL USB to serial UART breakout board
Usage examples:
  • Communicate between a PC and a microcontroller that doesn't have USB (ie Microchip PIC)
  • Upload a sketch to an Arduino without a USB port (ie Pro Mini)
  • Flash custom firmware like OpenWRT onto a wireless router

Product Specification

 
  • USB: Mini-USB female port for power and connection to PC/USB device
  • 6 male header pinss: DTR, RX, TX, VCC, CTS and GND to connect to serial device
  • Jumper: Select between 3.3V and 5V for the serial device
  • Fuse: 500mA self-restoring polyfuse
  • LED: RX/TX indicator LED
  • Dimensions: 43x17mm
  • Pin pitch: 2.54mm (0.1in)
